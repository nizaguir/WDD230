Target Audience

The target audience for this website is prospective visitors or Idaho residents of the cities of Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven. Parties who are looking to fund or support the city, or just curiosity seekers. The site is perfect for those with small businesses and would like to advertise on their cities page. The primary users of the website are looking to find the daily forecast, along with blog posts about the cities news and or upcoming events.

Scenario 1

Angela is a busy mother but tries to stay active by going on runs with her friend. While browing the Todaywillbe website she saw an ad / blog post about a new cycling building opening up in three days. She was so excited that she went and told all her friends so they could schedule a time to attend a class. Luckely, she and her friends were able to sign up as the spaces were taken up quiickly!

Scenario 2

Angela is looking to forward to her birthday on Saturday, becuase for the first time her family will be going on a camping trip. She wanted to stay close and not go too far so she went to the Todaywillbe website and check out the forecast for the nearest city of Preston, Idaho. According to the forecast it will be sunny, which is perfect for camping and going to the lake. She told her kids to pack for swimnsuits and not forget the suncreeen.

Scenario 3

Elle, is new in town. Her husband was born and raised in Preston, Idaho but this would be the first time living there. She always had a dream of opening a boutique so they decided Preston would be a good place. She did some research on the Todaywillbe website and found that there were only two boutiques in town. She was able to find a place for rent through the Todaywillbe website and she got all her merchandise. After a few months she wa ready to open up her boutique but she didnt know how to advertise. She remember how helpful was the Todaywillbe website so she went ahead and sent her infomration so it could be advertise in the website. On her grand opening she had more than 200 people check out her boutique

Scenario 4

Elle would like to have a website for her boutique. She realized that it would be helpful because a lot of times her customers wouldnt know the times her store was opened and there was no way to know unless you physically go to the store. She decided to post a hiring ad on the Todaywillbe website and recieve many applications to great canditates for this position.

Scenario 5

Elle and Angela met at the grand opening of Elle boutique. Elle expressed how she would like to start a fundraiser event for the foster care program in town. Angela knows a lot of people in town she talked to a few of them so they could sponsor this event. They also advertise the event at the Todaywillbe website and were able to raise 10k from just donations through their website.
