Target Audience

The target audience for this website is prospective visitors or Idaho residents of the cities of Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven. Parties who are looking to fund or support the city, or just curiosity seekers. The site is perfect for those with small businesses and would like to advertise on their cities page. The primary users of the website are looking to find the daily forecast, along with blog posts about the cities news and or upcoming events.
